Welcome to the Cooper Hewitt Digital Collection prototype, Collections Access and Transformative Stewardship!

The Collections Access and Transformative Stewardship (CATS) project aims to address Cooper Hewittâ€™s growing need for an accessible, digital subsite to investigate preserving and presenting interactive, born-digital works, with the goal of enabling the long-term stewardship of these works. CATS has two intertwined goals 1) Thought leadership to socialize and redefine terms related to access to collections (especially digital collections) and 2) an interactive digital platform that sets a new higher standard for accessibility.

This is the initial demo site to experience works in the collection currently without accessibilty features.
